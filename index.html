GAME DESCRIPTION:

Commando is a shoot 'em up, vertically scrolling arcade game that was originally released in 1985.

The main character is a soldier named Super Joe. When the game starts, he is dropped off in a jungle by a helicopter and he starts running to get out of the jungle upwards facing a massive assault of enemy soldiers.

He is armed with a sub-machine gun with unlimited ammunition and a limited supply of hand grenades which can kill several enemies at once. The game has a variety of levels and the maximum amount of players is 2.
